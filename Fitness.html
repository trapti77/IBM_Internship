Exercise Specialist Suggestions

  • Start Slow: Begin with low-intensity exercises and gradually increase the intensity.
  • Consistency is Key: Regular exercise is more beneficial than sporadic intense workouts.
  • Listen to Your Body: Avoid pushing yourself too hard and rest when needed.
  • Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of water before, during, and after exercise.
